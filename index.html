Projects

My "mock" Phone Store is one of my most recent projects. The store is built using only ReactJS (besides styling, which included minimal React Bootstrap, but mostly my own CSS). Honestly, this project was a lot of fun! When you first open the app, you're presented with an array of unique products available to "purchase". You can either click on the product, which will take you to a "details" page for the specific product you're interested in, or you can "add" the product to your cart in the lower-right corner. After shopping around you can check your cart, where you can see how much you owe (tax included!). However, if the total price is too high, don't stress! You can either remove individual products from your cart, or clear it entirely.

This real-time Crypto Tracker communicates with an API to retrieve and display the image, name, symbol, price, volume, any price changes, as well as the market cap for present-day cryptocurrencies. This neat app was again made with ReactJS, as well as Axios for retrieving the JSON data. If your friend is freaking out about a cryptocurrency and you have no idea why, utilize the search feature to quickly pull up that currency to understand why he's jumping up and down so much.

Are you falling behind schedule constantly? Are you wondering "If only there was a way to organize my daily tasks"? Well, do I have an app for you! My guaranteed-to-work Task Manager was built for this exact purpose! This colorful task management app allows you to add unlimited daily tasks, edit any of them for whatever reason, as well as crossing out the tasks you've completed AND remove individual tasks so you know how much you've accomplished during the day! Pretty neat huh? Tip: Clicking on the text of the task will cross out the text.
